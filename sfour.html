Common FAQs

Do you sell new or used cars?

We sell quality, pre-owned vehicles at affordable prices.

Are your cars inspected before sale?

Yes, all vehicles are inspected to ensure they meet safety and quality standards.

Do your vehicles have clean titles?

Yes, every vehicle we sell has a clean title with no salvage or flood damage.

Do your salespeople work on commission?

No. Our associates do not work on commission, so our goal is to help you find the best car, not pressure you into a sale.

Can I get financing through Will's Auto Sales?

Yes, we offer financing options to help make purchasing a vehicle more accessible.

Can I test drive a vehicle?

Absolutely. Test drives are encouraged so you can feel confident in your choice.

Are there hidden fees?

No. Our pricing is upfront and transparent.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Yes, trade-ins are welcome and evaluated fairly.
